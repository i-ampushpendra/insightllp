Privacy Policy

Your privacy is critically important to us. Read our policy to understand how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which **Newinsight Solutions LLP** collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users (each, a "User") of the https://www.newinsightsolutions.com website ("Site"). This privacy policy applies to the Site and all products and services offered by Newinsight Solutions LLP.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal identification information from Users in a variety of ways.

Information You Provide

  • - Full Name, Contact Details, Email
  • - Date of Birth and Nationality
  • - Educational Background
  • - Employment and Work Experience
  • - Family Information
  • - Test Scores (IELTS, PTE, etc.)
  • - Payment Information
  • - Any other information you provide directly

Automated Information

  • - IP address and browser type
  • - Device type and operating system
  • - Pages viewed and links clicked
  • - Date and time of visit
  • - Referring website or search queries
  • - Geographical location

How We Use Your Information

For Service Delivery

We use the information to provide our immigration and visa consultancy services, including eligibility assessments, document processing, and application submissions.

For Communication

We may use your email to send you updates about your case, newsletters, and promotional materials. You can opt out at any time.

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We will not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others.

With Your Consent

We may share your information with third parties when we have your explicit consent to do so. This is typically for purposes of visa application with government authorities or for third-party services like skills assessment bodies.

For Legal Purposes

We may disclose your information to comply with legal obligations, enforce our policies, respond to government requests, or protect our rights and property.

Data Protection & Security

We adopt appropriate data collection, storage, and processing practices and security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information.

Security Measures

  • - SSL encryption on our website
  • - Secure servers and databases
  • - Access control measures
  • - Regular security audits

Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and to comply with our legal obligations. We will securely destroy or de-identify personal information when it is no longer required.

Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to control your personal information. We are committed to providing you with the tools to exercise these rights.

Right to Access

You can request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.

Right to Rectification

You can ask us to correct or update any incomplete or inaccurate information.

Right to Erasure

You can request us to delete your personal information, subject to legal obligations.

Right to Restriction

You can ask us to limit the processing of your data under certain circumstances.

Right to Data Portability

You can request your data in a machine-readable format.

Right to Object

You can object to our processing of your personal information, especially for direct marketing.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

Newinsight Solutions LLP has the discretion to update this privacy policy at any time. We encourage Users to frequently check this page for any changes. You acknowledge and agree that it is your responsibility to review this privacy policy periodically and become aware of modifications.

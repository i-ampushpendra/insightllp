Australian Points System

The Australian skilled migration system uses a points-based assessment. You need a minimum of 65 points to be eligible, but higher scores improve your chances of getting an invitation.

Application Process

1

Skills Assessment: Get your skills assessed by the relevant Australian authority as per your occupation.

2

English Test: Take an approved English language test (IELTS, PTE, TOEFL) to demonstrate proficiency.

3

Expression of Interest: Submit EOI through SkillSelect with all required documents and information.

4

Invitation to Apply: Receive invitation and submit visa application within 60 days with supporting documents.

5

Assessment & Decision: DIBP processes application and issues visa if all checks are cleared.

Benefits of Australian PR

Live and work permanently in Australia

Access to Medicare (healthcare system)

Sponsor eligible relatives for permanent residence

Apply for Australian citizenship after 4 years

Children of PR holders get free education

Travel freely to and from Australia

Comprehensive Ranking System (CRS)

Canada's Express Entry uses the CRS to rank candidates. Your score is based on factors like age, education, work experience, and language proficiency. Higher scores increase your chances of receiving an Invitation to Apply (ITA).

Age 110 points max
Education 150 points max
Language Proficiency 160 points max
Work Experience 80 points max
Adaptability 100 points max

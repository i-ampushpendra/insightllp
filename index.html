Our Services

Permanent Residency

Permanent Residency Visa Obtain a Permanent Residence visa for a variety of nations by utilising several immigration schemes that are best appropriate for your present profile.

Job Assistance

Job Assistance We will assist you with the immigration process to your desired country. Through our existing contacts, we may also aid you with a passive job hunt in a foreign country.

Business Visa

A business visa is an official travel authorization intended for individuals engaging in business activities in a foreign country for a limited duration, without entering its labor market.

Legal Consultation

A legal consultation is a discussion with a lawyer to get advice on a legal matter. You can obtain legal consultation through online platforms like LawRato and Legalkart by submitting a question or scheduling a virtual meeting.

Tourist Visa

A tourist visa is an official document that authorizes a person to enter a foreign country for a limited, recreational period, typically for leisure, tourism, or visiting family.

Study Visa

When applying for a student visa, applicants must submit various documents and follow specific procedures, which can include composing a statement of purpose or cover letter

Post Landing Services

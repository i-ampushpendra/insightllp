Path to Settlement

Long-term residence and citizenship options in the UK

Indefinite Leave to Remain (ILR)

Permanent settlement after 5 years continuous residence (depending on route).

  • Requirements depend on visa route
  • Life in the UK test
  • Good character requirement

British Citizenship

Full citizenship after 1 year with ILR or 5 years residency (route dependent).

  • Residence & immigration history checks
  • Good character requirement
  • English & Life in the UK test

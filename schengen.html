Required Documents

  • Application form with photograph
  • Passport valid for at least 3 months
  • Travel medical insurance
  • Proof of accommodation and flight tickets
  • Financial solvency (bank statements)
  • Cover letter and travel itinerary
  • Proof of civil status

Expert Tips

  • Apply 3 months before your trip.
  • Ensure your passport has at least two blank pages.
  • Book refundable flights and accommodation.
  • Provide a detailed and logical travel itinerary.
  • Get a letter from your employer or school.
  • Be prepared for an interview at the consulate.

Application Process

1

Document Preparation: Our team helps you compile all required documents to ensure your application is complete.

2

Appointment Booking: We book your visa appointment with the relevant embassy or visa application center.

3

Application Submission: You submit your documents in person on the appointed date with our support.

4

Biometrics & Interview: Provide your fingerprints and a photo at the visa center. You may be required for a brief interview.

5

Processing & Decision: Your application is processed, and you are notified once a decision has been made.
